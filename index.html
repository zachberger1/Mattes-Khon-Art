Mattis Cohen Art

Mattis Cohen is a Jerusalem artist, specializing in copper art with a specific focus on menorah designs. Every menorah is completely individual in its style and structure. Each design is carefully handmade, making every single menorah a uniquely exquisite piece of art with its own distinct personality. This singular and exclusive artist also makes beautiful and striking vases, as well as lifesize replicas of stylized monograms for events.
